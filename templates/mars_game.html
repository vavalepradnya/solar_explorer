📊 Why is this composition critical?

Mars is a cold desert today primarily because its atmosphere is 100 times thinner than Earth's. While it is 95% CO₂, the sheer lack of atmospheric mass means it cannot trap enough heat. Increasing Oxygen alone wouldn't help humans because the low pressure would still be lethal!
